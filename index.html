Understanding DTC Categories

Powertrain (P)

Engine, transmission, and fuel system issues. Most common category.

Body (B)

Comfort systems, airbags, climate control, and body electronics.

Chassis (C)

ABS, traction control, suspension, and steering systems.

Network (U)

Communication errors between control modules and CAN bus.
