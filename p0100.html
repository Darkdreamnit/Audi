P0700

Transmission Control System Malfunction

Difficulty
Medium–High
Repair Time
1–4 hours
Cost
$150–$1500+
Severity
High

Why This Matters

The P0700 code indicates the transmission control system has detected a fault and triggered the check engine light. This is a gateway code, meaning deeper transmission issues are present and must be diagnosed before serious damage occurs.

Symptoms

  • ✔ Check Engine Light and limp mode
  • ✔ Harsh or delayed shifting
  • ✔ Transmission stuck in gear
  • ✔ Poor fuel economy
  • ✔ Stalling or hesitation

Causes

  • • Faulty solenoids
  • • Bad TCM
  • • Wiring issues
  • • Low or dirty fluid
  • • Internal transmission damage

How to Fix

  • ✔ Scan for additional codes
  • ✔ Repair wiring
  • ✔ Replace solenoids
  • ✔ Service transmission fluid
  • ✔ Replace or reprogram TCM

Step-by-Step Diagnosis

  1. Scan for additional transmission codes
  2. Check fluid condition
  3. Inspect wiring
  4. Test TCM
  5. Test solenoids
  6. Clear codes and retest

💡 Pro Tip

P0700 is only a warning flag. Always scan deeper transmission modules to find the real fault.
