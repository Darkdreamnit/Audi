P0700

Transmission Control System Malfunction

Difficulty
Medium–High
Repair Time
1–4 hours
Cost
$150–$1500+
Severity
High

Detailed Overview

The P0700 code indicates that the transmission control module has detected a fault and requested the check engine light. This is a general code and requires retrieving additional transmission-specific codes to identify the exact issue.

Step-by-Step Diagnosis

  • 🔧 Scan for additional transmission codes
  • 🔧 Check transmission fluid condition
  • 🔧 Inspect wiring and connectors
  • 🔧 Test TCM power and ground
  • 🔧 Evaluate solenoids and sensors

Cost Breakdown

DIY: $150 – $400
Independent Shop: $300 – $900
Dealership: $600 – $1500+

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I drive with P0700?

Driving is not recommended due to potential transmission damage.

What causes P0700?

Common causes include solenoids, wiring issues, or internal transmission faults.

Is P0700 serious?

Yes, it indicates a transmission issue that can worsen quickly.
