P0700

Transmission Control System Malfunction

Difficulty
Medium–High
Repair Time
1–4 hours
Cost
$150–$1500+
Severity
High

Why This Matters

The P0700 code indicates your Audi’s transmission system has detected a fault serious enough to trigger the Check Engine Light. While the engine may still run, the transmission is at risk. Continued driving without diagnosing the issue can lead to slipping gears, overheating, or even complete transmission failure—one of the most expensive repairs on any vehicle.

Symptoms

  • ✔ Check Engine Light and limp mode activation
  • ✔ Harsh, delayed, or erratic shifting
  • ✔ Transmission stuck in one gear
  • ✔ Reduced fuel efficiency
  • ✔ Vehicle may stall or hesitate

These symptoms occur because the transmission control module limits performance to prevent further damage.

Causes

  • • Failed transmission solenoids
  • • Faulty Transmission Control Module (TCM)
  • • Damaged wiring or connectors
  • • Low or contaminated transmission fluid
  • • Internal transmission damage

Because P0700 is a general fault, it almost always appears alongside more specific transmission codes.

How to Fix

  • ✔ Scan for additional transmission codes
  • ✔ Replace faulty solenoids
  • ✔ Repair wiring or connectors
  • ✔ Change transmission fluid
  • ✔ Replace or reprogram TCM

Step-by-Step Diagnosis

  1. Scan for additional codes (critical step)
  2. Check transmission fluid condition
  3. Inspect wiring harness and connectors
  4. Test TCM power and ground
  5. Evaluate solenoids and sensors
  6. Clear codes and road test

💡 Pro Tip

P0700 alone does NOT tell you what’s broken. Always scan with a tool that can read Audi/VW transmission modules—otherwise you're guessing and may replace the wrong parts.

FAQ

Can I drive with P0700?

You can, but it's risky. Limp mode and internal damage may occur.

Is P0700 expensive to fix?

Costs vary widely—from fluid service to full transmission rebuild.

Does P0700 mean transmission failure?

Not always, but it indicates a serious issue that must be diagnosed.
