P0700

Transmission Control System Malfunction

Difficulty
Medium–High
Repair Time
1–4 hours
Cost
$150–$1500+
Severity
High

Why This Matters

P0700 means your Audi’s transmission control module has detected a fault and requested the check engine light. This is not a specific failure, but a warning that deeper transmission issues exist. Ignoring it can lead to severe damage including slipping gears or complete transmission failure.

Symptoms

  • ✔ Check engine light + limp mode
  • ✔ Harsh or delayed shifting
  • ✔ Stuck in one gear
  • ✔ Poor fuel economy
  • ✔ Possible stalling

Causes

  • • Faulty transmission solenoids
  • • Bad TCM
  • • Wiring issues
  • • Low/dirty fluid
  • • Internal damage

How to Fix

  • ✔ Scan for secondary codes
  • ✔ Inspect fluid condition
  • ✔ Repair wiring
  • ✔ Replace solenoids
  • ✔ Replace/reprogram TCM

Step-by-Step Diagnosis

  1. Scan for additional transmission codes
  2. Check transmission fluid
  3. Inspect wiring harness
  4. Test TCM power/ground
  5. Evaluate solenoids
  6. Clear codes and retest

💡 Pro Tip

Always use a scanner that can read Audi transmission modules. A basic scanner will only show P0700 and hide the real issue.
