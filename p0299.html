P0299

Turbocharger Underboost Condition

Difficulty
Intermediate to Advanced
Repair Time
1–4 hours
Cost
$100–$2000+
Severity
High

Why This Matters

Underboost conditions significantly reduce engine performance and often indicate turbocharger issues or boost leaks. Ignoring this can lead to complete turbo failure.

Symptoms

  • ✔ Loss of power
  • ✔ Limp mode
  • ✔ Check engine light
  • ✔ Hissing noise
  • ✔ Weak turbo boost

Causes

  • • Wastegate actuator wear (common Audi issue)
  • • Faulty diverter valve
  • • Boost leaks
  • • Turbocharger failure
  • • Faulty boost sensor

How to Fix

  • ✔ Tighten wastegate linkage
  • ✔ Replace diverter valve
  • ✔ Repair boost leaks
  • ✔ Replace faulty sensor
  • ✔ Replace turbo if needed

💡 Pro Tip

Wastegate linkage wear is one of the most common causes of P0299 on Audi engines. Always check this before replacing the turbo.
