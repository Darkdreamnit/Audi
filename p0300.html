AudiCodes

P0300

Random/Multiple Cylinder Misfire

Detailed Overview

The P0300 code indicates random or multiple cylinder misfires. On Audi engines, this is often related to ignition coil failure, worn spark plugs, or fuel delivery issues. Turbocharged Audi engines amplify misfire conditions due to higher cylinder pressures.

Step-by-Step Diagnosis

  • 🔧 Scan and confirm P0300 code
  • 🔧 Inspect spark plugs and coils
  • 🔧 Check fuel injectors
  • 🔧 Inspect vacuum lines
  • 🔧 Run compression test

Common Mistakes

  • ⚠️ Replacing parts without diagnosis
  • ⚠️ Ignoring coil pack issues
  • ⚠️ Skipping fuel checks
