AudiCodes

P0300

Random/Multiple Cylinder Misfire

Difficulty
Medium
Repair Time
1–3 hours
Cost
$150–$800
Severity
High

Why This Matters

Multiple cylinder misfires can damage your catalytic converter and lead to severe engine issues.

Symptoms

  • ✔ Rough idle
  • ✔ Engine shaking
  • ✔ Poor acceleration

Causes

  • • Faulty spark plugs
  • • Bad ignition coils
  • • Fuel system issues

How to Fix

  • ✔ Replace spark plugs
  • ✔ Replace ignition coils
  • ✔ Check injectors

Frequently Asked Questions

Driving is not recommended for extended periods.
