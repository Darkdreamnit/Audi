P0302

Cylinder 2 Misfire Detected

Difficulty
Medium
Repair Time
1–3 hours
Cost
$150–$800
Severity
High

Detailed Overview

The P0302 code indicates a misfire in cylinder 2. On Audi engines, this is commonly caused by a failing ignition coil, worn spark plug, or a faulty fuel injector specific to that cylinder. Turbocharged Audi engines are particularly sensitive to ignition inconsistencies due to higher cylinder pressures.

Step-by-Step Diagnosis

  • 🔧 Scan and confirm P0302 code
  • 🔧 Swap ignition coil with another cylinder
  • 🔧 Inspect spark plug condition
  • 🔧 Check fuel injector for cylinder 2
  • 🔧 Perform compression test

Common Mistakes

  • ⚠️ Replacing parts without confirming the faulty cylinder
  • ⚠️ Ignoring ignition coil issues
  • ⚠️ Skipping injector testing
  • ⚠️ Not checking compression

Cost Breakdown

DIY: $50 – $200
Independent Shop: $200 – $600
Dealership: $400 – $1000

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I drive with P0302?

Driving is not recommended as continued misfires can damage the catalytic converter.

What causes P0302?

Common causes include faulty spark plugs, ignition coils, or fuel injectors.

How much does it cost to fix P0302?

Repairs typically range from $150 to $800 depending on the root cause.
