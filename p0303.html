P0303

Cylinder 3 Misfire Detected

Difficulty
Medium
Repair Time
1–3 hours
Cost
$150–$800
Severity
High

Detailed Overview

The P0303 code indicates a misfire in cylinder 3. On Audi engines, this is commonly caused by a failing ignition coil, worn spark plug, or a faulty fuel injector specific to that cylinder.

Step-by-Step Diagnosis

  • 🔧 Scan and confirm P0303 code
  • 🔧 Inspect spark plug and ignition coil
  • 🔧 Swap ignition coil with another cylinder
  • 🔧 Check fuel injector for cylinder 3
  • 🔧 Perform compression test

Common Mistakes

  • ⚠️ Replacing parts without proper diagnosis
  • ⚠️ Ignoring coil failures
  • ⚠️ Skipping fuel system checks

Cost Breakdown

DIY: $50 – $200
Independent Shop: $200 – $600
Dealership: $400 – $1000

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I drive with P0303?

Driving is not recommended as misfires can damage the catalytic converter.

What causes P0303?

Ignition coil failure, worn spark plugs, or injector issues.

How much does it cost to fix P0303?

Typically $150–$800 depending on the repair.
